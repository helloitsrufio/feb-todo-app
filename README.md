# THE MOST BAREBONES TODO APP YOU'LL EVER SEE
## WHAT IS THIS PROJECT?

A barebones todo app. 

Rufio wanted it to be **almost** as simple as possible; **almost** in the way that it is Typescript, not Javascript. But everything else is vanilla, no frameworks or anything. Just pure _logic_ and _suffering._

## WHY IS THIS PROJECT?
Rufio lowkey is afraid of making projects because she's not confident in her abilities. 
She also has yet to make a todo app.

**!HOWEVER!** She has worked professionally as a dev, and has done a metric ton of [Codewars challenges](https://www.codewars.com/users/helloitsrufio), and with all this experience, figured that, with Twitch chat's help, she could face her fears and make a todo app [on stream](https://twitch.tv/helloitsrufio).

🎉🎉🎉 THIS IS THAT APP 🎉🎉🎉

## "BUT IT'S HIDEOUS."

She knows, baby. The focus of this app was purely on the logic, not the looks. 

## "BUT THE LOGIC ISN'T GREAT EITHER."
This is a beginner project. The hope was to learn, and to understand what was going on, how to actively use JS/TS, interact with the DOM and HTML elements, etc. And fortunately, that hope was realized!

That also means that the code isn't best practice, or beautiful, etc. Sometimes you've just gotta do the thing, and then go back later and improve it.

## "OK...SO WILL RUFIO GO BACK LATER?"
Well...probably not. You see, now that she's conquered her fear of projects, there's a whole world of projects out there to do, and she is probably doing one of those instead :)
## TECHNOLOGIES USED
Typescript, HTML. Localstorage for the DB.
